About me

My name is Lyudmila Boroshovich and I deal with modern embroidery. I like handicrafts, in my free time I usually create something. For as long as I can remember, haberdashery and stationery have been my favorite places to shop. I have learned to embroider on my own and I am still perfecting various techniques. I embroider in hoops, which then serve as frames for my paintings. I show a new approach to embroidery, in which there are no rigid rules as in cross-stitch. I treat embroidery more like painting with paints, where each thread, just like a brush stroke, has the right to go its own way. I am inspired by nature and contemporary interiors. I usually embroider my patterns first and then draw them in a graphics program. Embroidering is my everyday life and one of the methods of expressing myself.

 

Galleries of embroidered works framed in their wonderful hoops are a dream of many a fan of cozy hygge-style interiors. Such embroidered images inspired the talented Lyudmila Boroshovich, who decorates her hoops mainly with floral motifs. Fern leaves, flowers, forest landscapes and picturesque houses fit perfectly into the current interior design trends
